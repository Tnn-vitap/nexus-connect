Nexus Connect: Freshers Unite

An Interactive Event for Freshers

Event Overview

"Nexus Connect: Freshers Unite" is a 2.5 to 3-hour interactive event designed to bring freshers together through engaging and tech-themed activities. The event includes games, quizzes, and creative challenges with a strong emphasis on fun and interaction.

Date: 11 October 2024

Time: 3:00 PM - 6:00 PM (IST)

Venue: Newton Hall

Join us for a fun-filled day where freshers can bond, collaborate, and showcase their creativity in a welcoming and inclusive environment.

Event Objectives

  • To create an interactive space for freshers to connect with each other and the organizing team.
  • To promote team-building, collaboration, and creativity through tech-themed activities.
  • To break the ice and foster lasting connections among new students.
  • To promote club ideals and teamwork through the event.

Event Flow

  1. Byte-Sized Stories: A short video highlighting the goals and activities of Team Next Nexus, followed by a playful introduction of team members to welcome freshers.
  2. Speed Networking: Freshers interact with each other in 10-minute sessions, sharing their names, departments, and interests to help form teams for the upcoming games.
  3. Truths and a Tech Lie – The Myth Busting Quiz: Teams are given two true tech statements and one false one. The goal is to identify the tech lie, challenging common myths in the tech world.
  4. Build A Mascot: Teams use an AI image generator (like DALL·E) to create a mascot representing their group, encouraging creativity and introducing AI design potential.
  5. Tech Trivia Showdown: An interactive trivia quiz featuring questions about AI applications, tech trends, and historical milestones.
  6. Echo Loop: A fun game where participants build and memorize a sequence of words and try to repeat them in the correct order after a certain period.
